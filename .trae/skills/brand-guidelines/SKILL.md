# Brand Guidelines (Anthropic)

To access Anthropic's official brand identity and style resources, use this skill.

## Brand Standards
- **Colors**:
  - Dark: `#141413` | Light: `#faf9f5` | Mid Gray: `#b0aea5`
  - Accents: Orange (`#d97757`), Blue (`#6a9bcc`), Green (`#788c5d`)
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: **Poppins** (24pt+, fallback: Arial)
  - Body: **Lora** (fallback: Georgia)

## Application Rules
- Apply Poppins to headings and Lora to body text.
- Use RGB color values for precise matching.
- Cycle through orange, blue, and green for non-text accents.
